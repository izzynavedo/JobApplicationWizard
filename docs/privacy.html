Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

Job Application Wizard is an open-source macOS and iOS application that helps you track job applications. Your privacy is important to us; this policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

Job Application Wizard does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All application data (job listings, notes, contacts, interview details, and tasks) is stored locally on your device.

Google Drive Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, the app requests access to the drive.appdata scope, which is limited to a private, app-specific folder in your Google Drive. This folder is not visible in your normal Drive file listing.

The app syncs the following structured data to your Google Drive app folder:

  • Job application metadata: company name, job title, URL, salary, location, status, and excitement rating
  • Contact metadata: name, email, phone
  • Interview round metadata: dates, format, interviewer names
  • Sub-task titles and completion status
  • Custom label names and colors
  • App preferences and settings

The following data is not synced and remains only on your device:

  • AI chat history
  • Full-text job descriptions
  • Full-text cover letters
  • Document raw text and bulk note content

Sync data is stored as JSON files in your Google Drive app-data folder using an append-only event log. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account permissions.

Authentication

Google sign-in uses OAuth 2.0 with PKCE (Proof Key for Code Exchange). Refresh tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain or macOS Keychain. We never see or store your Google password.

Third-Party Services

Other than the optional Google Drive integration, Job Application Wizard does not communicate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

AI Features

The app supports optional AI-assisted features. Any AI provider configuration (API keys, endpoints) is stored locally on your device. Conversations with AI providers are sent directly from your device to the provider you configure; no data passes through our infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Job Application Wizard is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Because the app is open source, you can also review changes via the GitHub repository.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
